How We Work

A straightforward process designed to solve your lighting control issues efficiently.

What Happens When You Hire Us

1. Initial Call & Scope Review

We start with a phone call or video meeting to understand your project status, specific issues, and timeline constraints.

You'll get an initial assessment of what's likely needed and a clear scope proposal.

2. Drawing & System Review

If applicable, we review electrical plans, control diagrams, submittals, and any existing documentation to identify potential issues before stepping on-site.

For troubleshooting calls, we may request photos or video of installed equipment to speed up diagnosis.

3. Coordination with Field Teams

We work directly with your electricians, project managers, or facilities staff — not as another layer of management, but as hands-on support.

Communication is direct, practical, and focused on solving the problem.

4. On-Site or Remote Support

Depending on the project, we provide either in-person field support or remote troubleshooting via phone, video, and photo sharing.

Remote support often resolves issues faster and at lower cost than unnecessary site visits.

5. Closeout & Documentation

After issues are resolved, you receive a summary of findings, corrective actions taken, and any recommendations for final commissioning or future maintenance.

Documentation is clear, concise, and ready to share with inspectors or commissioning agents.

Trust & Transparency
