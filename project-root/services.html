Our Services

Specialized support for lighting control projects that keeps installations on track.

Lighting Controls Troubleshooting

What it is: On-site and remote diagnosis of lighting control issues preventing commissioning or causing callbacks.

Who it's for: Electrical contractors stuck on non-responsive zones, communication failures, or unexpected system behavior.

Typical scenarios:

  • Devices won't connect or respond after installation
  • Zones not turning on/off as programmed
  • Intermittent failures or "ghost" issues
  • Title 24 compliance testing failures

Outcome: Clear root cause identification and step-by-step fixes to get the system operational.

On-Site & Remote Commissioning Support

What it is: Pre-commissioning reviews, field walkthroughs, and real-time support during final inspections.

Who it's for: General contractors and facility managers needing verification before the commissioning agent arrives.

Typical scenarios:

  • Pre-commissioning walkthroughs to catch issues early
  • Remote support during commissioning via video/photos
  • Documentation review (as-builts, submittals, testing logs)
  • Coordination with commissioning agents and inspectors

Outcome: Fewer commissioning delays, reduced punch list items, and smoother closeouts.

Contractor & Facility Training

What it is: Hands-on training for electrical crews and facilities teams on lighting control installation and operation.

Who it's for: Contractors wanting to reduce callbacks and facilities staff needing to maintain systems after turnover.

Typical scenarios:

  • Field crew training on proper installation techniques
  • Common wiring mistakes and how to avoid them
  • System operation training for facility maintenance staff
  • Custom training based on specific project systems

Outcome: Fewer installation errors, confident crews, and self-sufficient facility teams.

Independent System Review & Verification

What it is: Third-party review of lighting control plans, submittal drawings, and installed systems.

Who it's for: Project managers, owners' reps, and facilities seeking unbiased verification before acceptance.

Typical scenarios:

  • Plan review for Title 24 compliance and code adherence
  • Submittal review to catch issues before installation
  • Remote site walkthroughs via photos/video
  • Pre-acceptance system verification

Outcome: Independent confirmation that systems meet requirements before final payment.
