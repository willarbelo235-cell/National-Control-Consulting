Our Services

On-Site Consulting

We come to the job. We find the problems before commissioning does.

NCC provides hands-on, job-site consulting for commercial lighting controls projects. We review your installation against the control drawings, identify wiring issues, verify device placement, and give your crew a clear punch list before the commissioning agent ever walks through the door.

What's included:

  • Control drawing review and field verification
  • 0-10V wiring and polarity checks
  • Occupancy and daylight sensor placement review
  • Device addressing and zone verification
  • Pre-commissioning walkthrough report
  • Same-day findings summary delivered to EC and GC

Best for: Commercial and institutional projects over $50K in controls scope, or any project where a failed commissioning walkthrough would cause significant schedule or financial impact.

Electrician Training

The knowledge your crew needs. Delivered virtually, on your schedule.

NCC's virtual training courses are built specifically for working electricians — not engineers, not designers. Every course is field-focused, jargon-lite, and immediately applicable. No long certifications. No classroom seat time. Just the knowledge that prevents the mistakes that cost you money.

What's included:

  • Virtual live sessions via Zoom or Teams
  • On-demand recordings your crew can access anytime
  • Group pricing for companies with 5 or more seats
  • GC-sponsored sessions available for full subcontractor bases
  • Certificate of completion for each course
  • Pre-commissioning checklist included with every enrollment

Available courses cover: Lighting Controls 101, 0-10V Dimming, Sensor Placement, Networked Controls (DALI/BACnet), and Pre-Commissioning Field QA.

Project Retainers

Ongoing support from kickoff to closeout — without the overhead of a full-time controls engineer.

For EC companies running multiple controls projects at once, NCC offers monthly retainer agreements that give you on-call access to expert guidance throughout the project lifecycle. Think of it as having a controls specialist in your corner without adding headcount.

What's included:

  • Remote pre-construction drawing review
  • Ongoing phone and email support for field questions
  • Weekly or bi-weekly check-in calls (retainer level dependent)
  • Pre-commissioning remote walkthrough support
  • Priority scheduling for on-site visits if needed
  • Monthly summary report on open items and recommendations

Best for: EC companies running 3 or more active lighting controls projects simultaneously, or companies expanding into larger commercial controls work for the first time.
